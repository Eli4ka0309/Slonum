Information about the Olympiad
  • What will be the duration of the Olympiad?
    The duration will depend on the age of the student. For children in grades 1-2 - 1.5 hours; 3-6 grade - 2 hours; 6-9 grade - 2.5 hours
  • What will be the prizes and diplomas?
    Single cash prize in the form of 5.000$, also gifts from our sponsors
  • When and how long will the event take place?
    The Olympics will be held on 02/02/2021 at 12:00 am
  • How much will participation cost?
    Participation is free
